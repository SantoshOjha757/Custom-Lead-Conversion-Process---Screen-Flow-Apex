
**Project Description :**
Focuses to convert the Lead record into Account, Contact and Opportunity as well as optionally add the products on the Opportunity from Lead based on the condition.

**Objects include :**
Lead
Lead Product (Junction object)
Account
Contact
Opportunity
Opportunity Product
Price Book
Price Book Entry

**Project Requirement :**
The customer wanted to have options to add products during the lead process.
Once the lead is converted the same products should be added to the opportunity as opportunity product if the customer selected to create opportunity
